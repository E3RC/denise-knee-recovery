# Recovery Framework

This is the working recovery framework for the Denise knee dashboard, reminder runner, and future agent-assisted updates.

## Purpose

- Keep the caregiver workflow simple and phone-friendly.
- Drive the dashboard from recovery phases instead of scattered hard-coded tasks.
- Keep the reminder rhythm aligned with the hospital packet and current home-care goals.
- Keep private caregiver detail off the public family page.

## Current phase model

### Surgery day

- Focus on comfort, hydration, first movement, icing, meals, rest, and the first caregiver log.

### Early home recovery

- Days 2 through 7.
- Focus on short frequent walking, icing, elevation, daily exercises, incision watching, meals, hydration, and an evening log.

### Week 2

- Days 8 through 14.
- Continue the same daily rhythm while watching milestone progress and preparing for follow-up.

### Weeks 3 to 6

- Days 15 through 42.
- Continue rehab, monitor swelling and pain, and avoid jumping, twisting, running, heavy lifting, and strenuous yard work unless cleared.

### Longer recovery

- Day 43 and beyond.
- Focus on milestones, function, swelling trends, and follow-up questions.

## Reminder design

The active reminder schedule now follows this shape:

- Morning medication check
- Breakfast and hydration
- Morning walk
- Ice and elevate
- Mid-morning exercises
- Lunch and hydration
- Early afternoon walk
- Afternoon medication check
- Afternoon exercises
- Late afternoon ice and elevate
- Dinner and hydration
- Evening walk
- Evening recovery log
- Bedtime elevate and settle

## Red-flag guidance

These should remain caregiver-visible and not public-facing:

- Fever above 101
- Incision drainage, odor, or wound opening
- Bright red blood from the incision
- Calf or groin pain
- Excessive redness, warmth, or pus
- Pain not controlled with ice, elevation, rest, and medication

## Notes

- This framework is operational guidance and tracking only.
- It does not calculate medication doses.
- It does not replace surgeon or PT instructions.
- Project-manager and backup-nurse role notes live in `docs/agents/`.
